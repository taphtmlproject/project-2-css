Dreams Come True @ SVCE

    The Career Development Centre (CDC) acts as a bridge to build a fruitful symbiotic relationship 
    and association between an academic institution and the corporate world. The institute manages 
    students placement/internship through CDC and facilitates students to prepare, perform, take up 
    and excel in their leadership career in the best possible manner. CDC always works at its best to 
    attain the students dreams in respect of their career choice as well as to cope with the demands 
    of industries by offering multifaceted talents of great expertise.

    In 2022-23 alone, CDC brought more than 100+ companies onto campus for placements. Last year, the 
    highest offer made was 60 lakhs per year by Google. Over recent years, many reputed companies such 
    as Amazon, Google, Cisco, Wells Fargo, Walmart, DBS Asia, Yugabyte DB, Thought Works, Mindtree,
    HSBS, Yokogawa, Tejas Networks, Virtusa, Darwinbox, Meil, Hashedin, Infor, Infosys, Cognizant, 
    Wipro, Accenture, Capgemini, Deloitte, TCS, IBM, HCL, Tech Mahindra, Hexaware have participated
    in hiring.
